<header class="page-heading"><p class="eyebrow">Ongoing work</p><h1>Projects</h1><p>Longer bodies of work whose form may be academic, artistic, therapeutic or devotional.</p></header>

<div class="project-grid">
  <article class="project"><p class="field">Doctoral research</p><h3>Safeguarding Time</h3><p>A theological account of memory, judgement and responsibility in the Church after abuse, centred on the difference between procedural closure and moral or eschatological completion.</p></article>
  <article class="project"><p class="field">Visual art</p><h3>Record of Nighttime</h3><p>Drawings in graphite, ink and silverpoint. The work explores witness, residue and the marks by which an experience remains present.</p></article>
  <article class="project"><p class="field">Theology · Practice</p><h3>Sodality of Holy Saturday</h3><p>A developing devotional and theological project concerned with abiding, restraint and faithful presence where completion cannot be claimed.</p></article>
  <article class="project"><p class="field">Music · Prayer</p><h3>Motus Mariae</h3><p>An exploration of Marian song, modal practice and simple instruments as forms of recollection, accompaniment and prayer.</p></article>
