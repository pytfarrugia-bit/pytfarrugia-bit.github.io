The board

Click a fret to pluck it. Hold a key to sustain — melody 1–8, middle Q–I, bass A–K. Each fret shows its just interval above D.

The seven triads

The tonic Dm and the major III (F) and IV (G) lock against the drone; the others carry the comma.

Ear-first phrases

Drone-relative shapes to imitate by ear, not read. Set the drone, then loop a phrase or use call-and-response — it plays, then leaves an equal silence for you to answer on the wood.
